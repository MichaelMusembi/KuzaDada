About KuzaDada

KuzaDada was conceptualized as a digital solution to address the gender inequality that continues to hinder the potential of young girls worldwide, particularly in developing countries.

According to UNESCO (2022), 129 million girls are out of school globally, with socio-cultural barriers such as early marriages, gender roles, and financial constraints limiting their opportunities.

Our platform directly addresses the lack of accessible mentorship programs, career guidance, and digital learning opportunities that leave many young girls unprepared for professional success.
